Places to visit in Kottayam

Illikkal Kallu

Illikkal Kallu is a Thalanadu located on top of the Illickal Malaa in the Kottayam district of Kerala, India. The breathtaking aerial view of the surroundings and billows of mists that glide past shrouding the towering hill is sure to overwhelm you. The weather is cool and misty here. If the sky is clear, the Arabian Sea can be seen in the distant horizon as a thin blue line. Sunsets on full moon days are special here.

One of the best and highest waterfalls in Kottayam at a height of 200 feet. One has to trek through the middle of an estate, over the slippery rocky path, in order to reach the waterfalls. Marmala waterfall is about 40 meter in height, plunging into a 12-meter deep pool and feeds the River Teekoy. It might get dangerous around monsoons, best season to visit is during october where the water will be perfect to take a bath.

Elaveezhapoonchira

Ilaveezhapoonchira is a tourist destination located in Melukavu village. Awesome viewpoint in kottayam. Ilaveezha Poonchira is surrounded by three enchanting hillocks - Mankunnu, Kodayathoormala and Thonippara. This makes the place ideal for trekking. Perfect for a jeep safari or nature trek to this place. Sunrise and sunsets are best viewed here. Perfect for photography and to chill out with your loved ones.
